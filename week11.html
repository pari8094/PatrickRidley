Status Update

Last Week

  • We began pushing releases to Windfall Power for testing and evaluation. It was discovered that the iPhone Pro Max screen size scaled differently and did not look good.
  • We validated the issue on the simulator, recoded to correct it, but after pushing the fix, the issue still persisted (as of this morning).
  • We found a fellow employee with the same phone model, which allowed us to test on actual hardware — valuable for future testing.
  • Windfall liked the application and showed it to partners. We’re now planning to pitch a similar product already crafted to better fit their needs.
  • We began researching what's needed to start an LLC and other business essentials like a website and email setup.

This Week

  • Focused on cleaning up the application scaling issue, which remains a high priority for cross-device usability.
  • Took initial steps toward establishing the LLC, including research and planning.
  • Started collecting and reviewing new project requirements for future development.

Challenges & Impediments

  • A major concern was the app behaving correctly in the simulator but failing on real devices — raising questions about testing reliability.
  • Budget constraints limit our ability to test on multiple physical devices (iPhone & Android).
  • Fortunately, we borrowed a device, which helped us continue testing and progress.

Reflections & Recommendations

  • After discussions with Windfall, we recognize the benefit of incorporating a formal design phase into our process.
  • Tools like Figma — or even low-fi options like sketches — could help clarify layouts early in development.
  • We've struggled with:
    • Differences between light and dark modes
    • Vertical vs. horizontal layout adjustments
    • Frequent changes to logos and graphics
  • Establishing a unified design standard would streamline development and reduce UI rework.

Next Steps

  • Finalize the scaling fix and validate changes on physical devices.
  • Continue progressing with LLC formation tasks.
